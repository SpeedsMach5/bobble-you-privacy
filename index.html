Privacy Policy

Bobble You - Bobblehead Creator App

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Summary: Bobble You respects your privacy. We collect only what's necessary to make the app work, we don't sell your data, and you can delete your information at any time.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bobble You ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Fastwaters Technologies collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Bobble You mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Photos and Images: When you create bobbleheads, we temporarily process photos you select from your device's gallery or camera. These images are used solely for creating your bobblehead artwork.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and display name for authentication purposes.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure app compatibility and improve performance.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as features used, session duration, and crash reports to improve the App.
  • Sensor Data: The App uses your device's accelerometer to enable physics-based bobblehead animations. This data is processed locally and not stored or transmitted.

2.3 Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We receive confirmation of purchases but do not have access to your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To process your photos and create bobblehead images
  • Background Removal: To remove backgrounds from photos using our image processing services
  • Account Management: To manage your account and subscription status
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve features
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide and improve our App:

4.1 remove.bg (Kaleido AI)

We use remove.bg's API to remove backgrounds from photos. When you use background removal features, your image is sent to remove.bg servers for processing. Please review remove.bg's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

4.2 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication (if you create an account)
  • Analytics (anonymous usage statistics)
  • Crash reporting (to fix bugs)

Please review Firebase's Privacy Policy for more information.

4.3 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes purchase data to verify subscriptions. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

4.4 Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for various functions including ML Kit for face detection. Face detection is performed locally on your device.

4.5 Advertising

We may use advertising identifiers to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

5. Data Storage and Retention

5.1 Local Storage

Your created bobbleheads and saved projects are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your creations to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.

5.2 Cloud Storage

If you use cloud features, your data may be stored on secure servers. We retain this data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

5.3 Temporary Processing

Images sent to remove.bg for background removal are processed and immediately deleted from their servers. We do not retain copies of your photos on our servers.

5.4 Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months to analyze trends and improve the App.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data. To delete your data, contact us at the email below or use the "Delete Account" option in App settings.
  • Correction: You can update your account information through the App settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics collection in the App settings.
  • Data Portability: You can export your saved projects from the App at any time.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Bobble You is intended for users of all ages, including children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
  • The App can be used without creating an account or providing personal information.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
  • We will promptly delete any information we discover was collected from a child under 13 without proper consent.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's app usage and teach them about online privacy.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.
